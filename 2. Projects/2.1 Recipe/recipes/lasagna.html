Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food — layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey melted mozzarella stacked between sheets of tender pasta. It is a classic Italian-American dish that feeds a crowd and tastes even better the next day.

This recipe uses a simple homemade bolognese-style meat sauce and a three-cheese filling to create a hearty, satisfying meal that is perfect for Sunday dinners or meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain and set aside.
  3. Brown ground beef with garlic in a skillet over medium heat. Drain fat, then stir in marinara sauce and Italian seasoning. Simmer 10 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, half the mozzarella, and half the Parmesan. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 noodles over the sauce, then spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture, then 1/3 of the remaining meat sauce. Repeat layers twice more.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil and bake 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 20 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let rest 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
