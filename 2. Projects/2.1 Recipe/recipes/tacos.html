Tacos

Description

These classic ground beef tacos come together in under 30 minutes, making them a weeknight staple. Seasoned beef is piled into warm tortillas and topped with all your favorites — shredded cheese, fresh salsa, sour cream, and crisp lettuce.

Simple, customizable, and always a crowd-pleaser. Set up a taco bar and let everyone build their own.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 packet (1 oz) taco seasoning
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and cook, breaking it apart, until browned (about 7 minutes). Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in taco seasoning and water. Reduce heat to medium and simmer 3–4 minutes until sauce thickens.
  3. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds per side, or wrap in a damp paper towel and microwave 30 seconds.
  4. Spoon seasoned beef into each tortilla.
  5. Top with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, sour cream, and salsa as desired.
  6. Squeeze fresh lime juice over tacos before serving.
